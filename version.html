Why am I doing this

I chose to show the differnt versions of the website for two reasons. The first reason is for anyone inspiring to make a static website like this. They can see the progess I made whether it be visually, content wise, or organization wise. That you can learn how to do this and it apparently is not that hard. The second reason is for myself. I do not want to repeat mistakes that I have made in the past and having a record of the past a few clicks away is very handy. The third reason (I lied with just two reasons I'm #sorryNotSorry) is also for myself. I like to see the progress I made between jumps of learning. It will inspire me to keep pushing and learn more in the tough times.
